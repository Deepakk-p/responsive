Our Activities

Native Experience

Responsible Tourism (RT) Mission lets travellers experience the compassion and craftsmanship of the serene, secluded villages of Kerala up close. These villages of God's Own Country are blessed with many small-scale traditional industries and skilled artisans.

Cultural Experience

Kerala, a slice of green sandwiched between the Western Ghats and the Arabian Sea, is rich with art, artforms, rituals and festivals. The State owns a centuries-old history and has a long tradition in art and literature.

Village Life Experience

The soul of Kerala resides in the rustic terrains of villages that evoke fresh experiences on every visit. Village life of God's Own Country is a symphony composed on strings of beauty painted with myriad traditions and cultures.
